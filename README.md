## 🚀 AI Customer Success Workflow

An AI-powered Customer Success system designed to proactively identify churn risks, improve customer engagement, and drive product adoption through data and automation.

## 🧩 Problem

Customer Success teams often operate reactively, identifying risks too late due to limited visibility into customer behavior and fragmented data across tools.

## 🎯 Objective

Build a proactive system that enables early identification of at-risk customers and supports data-driven engagement to improve retention and adoption.

## 💡 Solution

This project demonstrates an AI-driven Customer Success workflow that:

Tracks customer engagement and activity
Calculates dynamic health scores
Identifies churn risks using behavioral signals
Generates actionable insights using AI
Triggers automated workflows for timely intervention


## ⚙️ How It Works

## 1. Data Tracking
Collects key customer signals such as logins, feature usage, and support interactions

## 2. Health Scoring
Assigns scores based on engagement and activity patterns

## 3. AI Insights
Analyzes data to identify risks and recommend next actions

## 4. Automated Actions
Triggers alerts, tasks, or outreach workflows for Customer Success teams

## 📈 Impact
Improves customer retention through early risk detection
Drives product adoption with targeted engagement
Reduces manual effort through automation
Creates scalable Customer Success processes
